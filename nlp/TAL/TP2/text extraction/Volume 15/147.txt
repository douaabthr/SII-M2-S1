Generalization of GCD matrices

And create a square matrix\( \left\langle S \right\rangle = (s_{ij} )_{{{\text{t}} \times {\text{t}}}} = (\gcd (d_{i} ,d_{j} ))_{{{\text{t}} \times {\text{t}}}} \)possessed the general greatest common factors\( \gcd (d_{i} ,d_{j} ) \)as arrays\( s_{ij} = \gcd (d_{i} ,d_{j} ) \).
