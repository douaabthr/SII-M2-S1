<s>
Learning

belong

the

class

machine

evolutionary

</s>
<s>
LCS

more

evolutionary

</s>
<s>
Recent
LCS

the

system

</s>
<s>
Pattern

</s>
<s>
New

</s>
<s>
However

s

</s>
