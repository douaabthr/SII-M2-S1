<s>
In

path

a
single
robot
and
multi-Robots
with
multi-objective
an
improved
ant
colony
algorithm
is
proposed
.
In
the
improved
ant
colony
algorithm
both
local
and
global
optimal
searching
are
considered
</s>
<s>
In
local
optimal
searching
gradient
distribution
is
used
to
initialize
the
pheromone
concentration
to
improve
the
convergence
speed
of
the
algorithm
</s>
<s>
The
self-adaptive
impact
factor
is
used
to
enhance
better
fitness
of
the
algorithm
in
different
environments
</s>
<s>
In
global
optimal
searching
a
reward

established
to

the
pheromone
update
strategy
</s>
<s>
Furthermore
a
node
rearrangement
strategy
is
adopted
to
increase
the
diversity
of
solutions
</s>
<s>
According
to
the
improved
colony
algorithm
the
problem
of
path
planning
of
a
single
robot
and
multi-Robots
with
multi-objective
can
be
solved
</s>
<s>
In
two
different

compared
experiments
show
that
the
improved
colony
algorithm
can
obtain
better
path
length
convergence
speed

running
time
than
traditional
ant
colony

ACO

ant
colony
algorithm
IACO-SFLA

robot
</s>
<s>
Simulation

improved
ant
colony
algorithm

ability

multi-objective

</s>
<s>
Especially
all

.
</s>
