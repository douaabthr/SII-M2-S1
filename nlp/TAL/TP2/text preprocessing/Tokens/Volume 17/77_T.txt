<s>

Pareto
Corner

Objective

in

</s>
<s>
Multi-objective
optimization
evolutionary
algorithms

is
one

the
most

approaches
for
solving
the
multi-objective
optimization
problems
MOPs
</s>
<s>
When
the
number

objectives

greater
than
three
the
MOPs
are
considered
as
many-objective
optimization
problem
MaOPs
and
many-objective
optimization
evolutionary
algorithms
MaOEAs
are
proposed
to
solve
MaOPs
</s>
<s>
However
MaOPs
often
contain
redundant
objectives
which
do
not
conflict
to
any
other
objectives
or
even
correlate
positively
to
some
other
</s>
<s>
These
redundant
objectives
seriously
degrade
the
efficiency
of
MOEAs/MaOEAs
so
they
should
be
removed
to
help
MaOEAs
work
better
</s>
<s>
Therefore
this
paper
proposes
new
objective
reduction
algorithms
which
uses
a
Pareto
corner
search
algorithm
PCSEA
to
generate
non-dominated
solutions
at
corners
of
Pareto
front
PF
and
then

machine
learning
techniques
to
remove
redundant
objectives
</s>
<s>
The
proposed
methods
not
only
promote
the
strengths
of
PCSEA
in
finding
non-dominated
solutions
but
also
promote
the
strengths
of
machine
learning
algorithms
in
automatically
finding
the
optimal
set
of
objectives
</s>
<s>
The
experiments
on
36
instances
of
DTLZ5
I
M
and
10
instances
of
WFG3
M
show
that
the
proposed
methods
can
more
often
find
the
right
set
of
objectives
than
six
other
benchmark
methods
respectively
about
80%
success
compared
to
about
62%
success
of
the
other
methods
.
</s>
