<s>
Multi-resolution
analysis
and
deep
neural
network
architecture
based
hybrid
feature
extraction
technique
for
plant
disease
identification
and
severity
estimation
</s>
<s>
The
crop
protection
techniques
using
efficient
plant
disease
identification
are
highly
beneficial
for
Smart
Agriculture
Management
which
further
decreases
the
gap
between
the
demand/supply
of
consumers
farmers
</s>
<s>
However
an
efficient
feature
extraction
technique
is
desirable
to
achieve
high
accuracy
least
graphical
processing
and
low
processing
time
for
coloured
images
</s>
<s>
Therefore
this
work
proposes
a
hybrid
novel
feature
extraction
technique
for
coloured
plant
leaf
images
to
overcome
these
challenges
</s>
<s>
This
work
is
divided
into
two
parts
1
</s>
<s>
Plant
disease
identification
and
2
</s>
<s>
Plant
disease
severity
estimation
</s>
<s>
In
this
technique
multiresolution
analysis
using
large-scale
coefficients
of
Discrete
Wavelet
Transform
DWT
is
performed
using
orthogonal
wavelets
</s>
<s>
The
orthogonal
wavelets
such
as
Daubechies
Biorthogonal
Symlets
and
Coiflets
with
appropriate
vanishing
moments
are
utilized
to
select
significant
features
</s>
<s>
Then
the
Eigen
decomposition
of
large-scale
DWT-based
coloured
image
features
is
utilized
using
Principal
Component
Analysis
PCA
where
only
the
Eigenvalues
with
large
variance
are
considered
for
further
feature
selection
</s>
<s>
Several
experiments
are
carried
out
for
the
selection
of
felicitous
sub-bands
decomposition
levels
</s>
<s>
The
Deep
Neural
network
DNNs
architectures
such
as
AlexNet
ResNet
101
and
Inception
V3
are
incorporated
to
classify
the
obtained
features
</s>
<s>
The
DNNs
operate
on
Convolutional
Neural
Networks
which
utilize
the
weights
from
pre-learned
ImageNet
Database
classes
to
classify
various
classes
</s>
<s>
The
combination
of
DNNs
with
Transfer
Learning
and
Fine
Tuning
is
made
to
tune
the
parameters
for
better
efficacy
</s>
<s>
Experiments
were
performed
on
the
PlantVillage
Database
which
contains
healthy/diseased
images
of
plants
such
as
Apple
Corn
Grapes
and
tomato
leaves
</s>
<s>
A
new
database
is
created
by
capturing
the
images
of
Rice
Crop
Leaves
including
healthy
and
diseased
leaves
</s>
<s>
A
total
of
8000
images
are
processed
for
disease
identification
</s>
<s>
The
proposed
technique
provided
99%
accuracy
for
disease
identification
task
and
90%
for
disease
severity
estimation
in
the
real-life
Rice
Dataset
and
outperformed
the
existing
techniques
in
this
field
of
research
.
</s>
