<s>
A
novel
approach
to
build
accurate
and
diverse
decision
tree
forest
</s>
<s>
Decision
tree
is
one
of
the
best
expressive
classifiers
in
data
mining
</s>
<s>
A
decision
tree
is
popular
due
to
its
simplicity
and
straightforward
visualization
capability
for
all
types
of
datasets
</s>
<s>
Decision
tree
forest
is
an
ensemble
of
decision
trees
</s>
<s>
The
prediction
accuracy
of
the
decision
tree
forest
is
more
than
a
decision
tree
algorithm
</s>
<s>
Constant
efforts
are
going
on
to
create
accurate
and
diverse
trees
in
the
decision
tree
forest
</s>
<s>
In
this
paper
we
propose
Tangent
Weighted
Decision
Tree
Forest
TWDForest
which
is
more
accurate
and
diverse
than
random
forest
</s>
<s>
The
strength
of
this
technique
is
that
it
uses
a
more
accurate
and
uniform
tangent
weighting
function
to
create
a
weighted
decision
tree
forest
</s>
<s>
It
also
improves
performance
by
taking
opinions
from
previous
trees
to
best
fit
the
successor
tree
and
avoids
the
toggling
of
the
root
node
</s>
<s>
Due
to
this
novel
approach
the
decision
trees
from
the
forest
are
more
accurate
and
diverse
as
compared
to
other
decision
forest
algorithms
</s>
<s>
Experiments
of
this
novel
method
are
performed
on
15
well
known
publicly
available
UCI
machine
learning
repository
datasets
of
various
sizes
</s>
<s>
The
results
of
the
TWDForest
method
demonstrate
that
the
entire
forest
and
decision
trees
produced
in
TWDForest
have
high
prediction
accuracy
of
1
7%
more
than
existing
methods
</s>
<s>
TWDForest
also
creates
more
diverse
trees
than
other
forest
algorithms
.
</s>
