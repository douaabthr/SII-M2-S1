<s>
Multi-view
fusion
for
recommendation
with
attentive
deep
neural
network
</s>
<s>
Recommendation
systems
have
been
widely
developed
and
introduced
in
numerous
applications
</s>
<s>
However
due
to
the
lack
of
sufficient
user
feedback
data
the
recommendation
performance
of
such
systems
is
often
affected
by
data
sparsity
</s>
<s>
To
address
this
problem
a
multi-view
fusion
recommendation
algorithm
with
an
attentive
deep
neural
network
is
proposed
</s>
<s>
A
two-stage
joint
learning
solution
is
designed
in
the
proposed
model
which
combines
user
attributes
item
attributes
and
user-item
interaction
information
into
a
unified
framework
</s>
<s>
The
convolutional
neural
network
and
attention
mechanism
are
applied
to
improve
effect
of
extracting
features
from
user
and
item
attributes
</s>
<s>
The
extended
deep
neural
network
model
based
on
matrix
factorization
and
multiple-layer
perception
is
used
to
enhance
the
feature
extraction
of
user
and
item
interaction
information
</s>
<s>
Experimental
results
on
the
MovieLens-1
M
and
Book-Crossing
real
datasets
show
that
the
proposed
algorithm
can
achieve
the
best
recommendation
accuracy
compared
with
other
classical
recommendation
algorithms
even
with
extremely
sparse
data
.
</s>
