<s>
Optimizing
performance
of
handling
machines
in
timber
worksites
</s>
<s>
With
an
intensive
growth
of
innovative
technologies
implementation
nowadays
the
main
problem
is
to
evaluate
the
respectability
of
the
used
technologies
and
increase
the
productivity
of
loading
and
transporting
operations
of
the
timber
industry
</s>
<s>
This
work
aimes
to
analyze
the
technological
and
economic
factors
affecting
the
productivity
and
cost
of
operation
of
modern
loading
machines
used
at
enterprises
in
Russia
</s>
<s>
Comparative
analysis
is
performed
between
the
front
loaders
of
Sennebogen
Liebherr
Volvo
Fuchs
with
articulated
steering
HighLift
loaders
and
full-circle
timber
material
handling
machines
</s>
<s>
The
results
of
the
analysis
of
the
loaders
physical
characteristics
show
that
at
increasing
the
machine
weight
by
1.5
times
operating
costs
increase
correspondingly
by
about
1.5
times
while
output
capacity
doubles
</s>
<s>
Stall
load
ratings
for
front
loaders
depending
on
the
position
of
the
bar
and
the
arm
span
and
the
optimal
conditions
of
efficient
functioning
and
cost
structure
for
these
types
of
loading
machines
are
established
</s>
<s>
The
obtained
results
can
be
used
for
future
scientific
research
and
applied
in
industrial
enterprises
as
technological
recommendations
.
</s>
