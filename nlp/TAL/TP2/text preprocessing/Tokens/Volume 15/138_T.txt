<s>
A
Kriging-based
multi-point
sequential
sampling
optimization
method
for
complex
black-box
problem
</s>
<s>
The
Generalized
Efficient
Global
Optimization
GEGO
algorithm
assisted
by
Kriging
model
can
solve
black-box
problem
of
complex
computing
</s>
<s>
However
a
single
sampling
point
obtained
in
each
iteration
process
may
cause
longer
objective-evaluation
time
and
slower
convergence
speed
in
contrast
with
multi-point
sampling
optimization
methods
</s>
<s>
For
this
a
Kriging-based
multi-point
sequential
sampling
optimization
KMSSO
method
is
presented
</s>
<s>
The
proposed
method
uses
uncertainty
estimate
information
of
Kriging
to
construct
the
multiple-point
generalized
Expected
Improvement
EI
criterion
</s>
<s>
In
optimization
cycle
this
criterion
is
maximized
to
produce
the
Pareto
front
data
which
will
be
further
screened
to
obtain
final
expensive
evaluation
points
</s>
<s>
For
numerical
tests
and
an
engineering
case
KMSSO
is
compared
to
GEGO
and
HAM
algorithm
and
is
shown
to
deliver
better
results
</s>
<s>
It
is
also
proves
that
when
multiple
points
are
added
per
cycle
the
optimization
accuracy
and
convergence
property
are
both
improved
.
</s>
