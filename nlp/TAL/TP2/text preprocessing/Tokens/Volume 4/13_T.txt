<s>
Parameter
control
of
metaheuristics
with
genetic
fuzzy
systems
</s>
<s>
This
paper
introduces
a
genetic
fuzzy
system
for
parameter
control
of
metaheuristics
</s>
<s>
Two
basic
metaheuristics
have
been
considered
as
examples
genetic
algorithm
and
tabu
search
</s>
<s>
The
controlled
parameters
of
the
tabu
search
are
the
short
and
long
term
memories
</s>
<s>
Parameters
of
the
genetic
algorithm
under
control
are
the
mutation
and
reproduction
rates
</s>
<s>
Fuzzy
rule-based
models
offer
a
natural
mechanism
to
describe
global
behavior
as
a
combination
of
control
rules
</s>
<s>
They
also
inherit
a
means
to
gradually
shift
between
control
rules
which
jointly
defines
a
control
strategy
</s>
<s>
They
are
a
natural
candidate
to
construct
parameter
control
strategies
because
they
provide
a
way
to
develop
decision
mechanisms
based
on
the
specific
nature
of
search
regions
and
transitions
between
their
boundaries
</s>
<s>
An
application
example
using
the
classic
vehicle
routing
problem
with
time
windows
is
included
to
evaluate
the
genetic
fuzzy
system
performance
</s>
<s>
Experimental
results
show
that
GFS-controlled
metaheuristics
improve
search
behavior
and
solution
quality
when
compared
against
standard
constant
parameters
genetic
and
tabu
search
approaches
</s>
<s>
It
also
provides
reasonably
good
suboptimal
solutions
faster
than
specially
tailored
exact
methods
reported
in
the
literature
.
</s>
