<s>
Genetic-based

for
cue

in

act

</s>
<s>
Automatic

</s>
<s>
The

specific

ranking
approaches
</s>
<s>
Despite
their
computational
efficiency

high
dimensional
domains
they
are
not
optimal

to
relevance
and
redundancy
</s>
<s>
In

we

a
genetic-based
approach
for
cue
phrase
selection
which

a
variable
length

algorithm
developed
to
cope
with
the
high
dimensionality
of
the
domain
</s>
<s>
We
evaluate
the
performance
of
the
proposed
approach
against
several
ranking
approaches
</s>
<s>
Additionally
we
assess
its
performance
for
the
selection
of
cue
phrases

phrase
s

and
phrase
s
position
</s>
<s>
The
results
provide
experimental
evidences
on
the
ability

the
genetic-based
approach
to
handle
the
drawbacks
of
the
ranking
approaches
and
to
exploit
cue
s
type
and
cue
s
position

to
improve
the
selection
</s>
<s>
Furthermore
we
validate
the
use
of
the
genetic-based
approach
for
machine
learning
applications
</s>
<s>
We
use
selected
sets
of
cue
phrases
for
building
a
dynamic
Bayesian
networks
model
for
dialogue
act
recognition
</s>
<s>
The
results
show
its
usefulness
for
machine
learning
applications
.
