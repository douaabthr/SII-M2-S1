<s>
Driver
drowsiness
detection
using
modified
deep
learning
architecture
</s>
<s>
This
paper
proposes
a
non-invasive
approach
to
detect
driver
drowsiness
</s>
<s>
The
facial
features
are
used
for
detecting
the
driver
s
drowsiness
</s>
<s>
The
mouth
and
eye
regions
are
extracted
from
the
video
frame
</s>
<s>
These
extracted
regions
are
applied
on
hybrid
deep
learning
model
for
drowsiness
detection
</s>
<s>
A
hybrid
deep
learning
model
is
proposed
by
incorporating
both
modified
InceptionV3
and
long
short-term
memory
LSTM
network
</s>
<s>
InceptionV3
is
modified
by
adding
global
average
pooling
layer
for
spatial
robustness
and
dropout
technique
to
prevent
overfitting
on
training
data
</s>
<s>
The
proposed
hybrid
model
is
compared
with
convolutional
neural
network
IncpetionV3
and
LSTM
over
NTHU-DDD
dataset
</s>
<s>
The
proposed
model
performs
better
than
the
other
model
in
terms
of
performance
measures
</s>
<s>
The
proposed
model
is
able
to
detect
driver
fatigue
effectively
.
</s>
