<s>
A

ECG

is

</s>
<s>
Specifically

PACO
finds
a
subset

nonzero
positions
of
a
sparse
wavelet
domain
ECG
signal
vector
that
is
used
for
the
reconstruction

the
signal
</s>
<s>
A
time

used

the
proposed
PACO
for

certain
decisions
of
the
ants
during
the
run
of
the
algorithm
</s>
<s>
The
optimization

of
the
PACO
is
compared
with
various
algorithms
from
the
literature
for
ECG
signal
reconstruction
and
with
two
random
search
heuristics
</s>
<s>
Experimental
results
are
presented
for
ECG
signals
from
the
MIT-BIT
Arrhythmia
database
</s>
<s>
The
influence
of
several
algorithmic
parameters
and
of
a
local
search
procedure
is
evaluated
</s>
<s>
The
results
show
that
the
proposed
PACO
algorithm
reconstructs
ECG
signals
with
high
accuracy
.
</s>
