<s>
On
the
cryptanalysis
of
S-DES
using
nature
inspired
optimization
algorithms
</s>
<s>
Cryptanalysis
has
emerged
to
be
an
important
topic
in
the
era
of
modern
emerging
technologies
</s>
<s>
The
cryptanalysis
of
Simplified
Data
Encryption
Standard
S-DES
is
a
NP-Hard
combinatorial
problem
</s>
<s>
This
paper
has
two
goals
</s>
<s>
Firstly
we
study
the
cryptanalysis
of
S-DES
via
nature-inspired
meta-heuristic
algorithms
namely
Cuckoo
Search
Firefly
and
Black-Hole
Optimization
Algorithms
</s>
<s>
Each
of
these
algorithms
is
based
on
fascinating
natural
phenomena
and
exploits
the
inherent
uniqueness
of
such
phenomena
to
solve
optimization
problems
</s>
<s>
Secondly
we
present
a
comparative
study
on
the
efficiency
of
these
three
fairly
new
algorithms
with
that
of
previously
established
Memetic
Algorithm
and
Genetic
Algorithms
in
regard
to
S-DES
cryptanalysis
</s>
<s>
Through
experimentations
and
extensive
tests
it
has
been
shown
that
the
proposed
algorithm
based
on
Cuckoo
Search
proves
to
be
most
efficient
with
respect
to
accuracy
and
execution
time
.
</s>
