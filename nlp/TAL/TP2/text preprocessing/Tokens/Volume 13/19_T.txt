<s>
Hybrid

algorithm
and
a
fuzzy
logic
classifier
for
heart
disease
diagnosis
</s>
<s>
For
the
past
two
decades
most
of
the
people
from
developing
countries
are
suffering

heart
disease
</s>
<s>
Diagnosing
these
diseases

stages

patients

the
risk

death

the
cost

</s>
<s>
The

adaptive
genetic

with
fuzzy
logic
AGAFL

heart
disease

will

medical

</s>
<s>
The

sets

classification

</s>
<s>
The
generated

the
adaptive
genetic
algorithm
</s>
<s>
First
important
features

heart
disease

selected
by
rough
set
theory
</s>
<s>
The
second
step
predicts
the
heart
disease
using
the
hybrid
AGAFL

</s>
<s>
The

the
publicly
available
UCI
heart
disease

</s>
<s>
Thorough
experimental
analysis
shows
that

approach

outperformed

existing
methods
.
</s>
