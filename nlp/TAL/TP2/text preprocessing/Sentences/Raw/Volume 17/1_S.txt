Preface special issue on intelligent planning and decision making
Driven by technological advances and global informatization the world continues to evolve at an unprecedented rate and intelligent planning and decision-making processes have never been more critical for adapting to these developments
In this era of information overload rapidly changing circumstances and complex systems the ability to make informed decisions and devise effective plans is essential
Whether in business healthcare finance environmental management or countless other fields there is an undeniable need for intelligent systems and methodologies
Machine learning ML and Artificial Intelligence AI are driving as some of the best tools for future intelligent system research
For capturing related theoretical and practical advances this specie issue Intelligent Planning and Decision Making is devoted to the recent and significant studies on ML AI evolutionary computing optimization algorithms and their applications for intelligent planning and decision making .
